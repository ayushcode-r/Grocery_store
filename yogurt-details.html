Product details--

Yogurt is a creamy and nutritious dairy product that has been consumed for centuries. It is made by fermenting milk with beneficial bacteria, typically Lactobacillus bulgaricus and Streptococcus thermophilus. This fermentation process converts lactose (milk sugar) into lactic acid, giving yogurt its tangy flavor and thick, smooth texture
